Hands-On Machine Learning

"Hands-On Machine Learning with Scikit-Learn, Keras, and TensorFlow" is an essential read for ML engineers looking to build practical skills. This book offers a perfect blend of theory and hands-on examples, guiding you through the entire machine learning process using popular libraries like Scikit-Learn and TensorFlow.

You'll learn how to build intelligent systems from scratch, covering a wide range of ML and deep learning techniques. The book is particularly valuable for its clear explanations of complex concepts and its focus on best practices in the field.

Author: Aurélien Géron

Published: 2019 (2nd Edition)

Key Topics: Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Scikit-Learn, TensorFlow, Neural Networks
